```

"How much are investors paying for each dollar

‚Ä¢ AAPL stock price: $182
‚Ä¢ AAPL earnings per share: $6.30
‚Ä¢ P/E ratio: 182 √∑ 6.30 = 29x

‚Ä¢ Investors expect strong growth
‚Ä¢ "Premium" valuation
‚Ä¢ Example: NVDA at 65x

‚Ä¢ Slower growth expected
‚Ä¢ "Value" stock
```
