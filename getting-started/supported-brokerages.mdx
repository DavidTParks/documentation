## Connect almost any brokerage

Tendrill uses Plaid to securely connect to your investment accounts. Plaid supports over **10,000 financial institutions** across the United States, including all major brokerages, banks with investment services, and retirement account providers.

<Info>
If your institution offers online account access, there's a good chance it works with Tendrill. Search for your brokerage during the connection process to confirm.
</Info>

## Not supported

Some account types have limitations:

<Warning>
**Currently not supported:**
- Cryptocurrency-only exchanges (Coinbase, Kraken, etc.) - stock holdings are supported
- Foreign brokerage accounts
- Some employer stock plans (ESPPs, RSUs may show as "other holdings")
- Annuity contracts
- Life insurance investment accounts
</Warning>

## How to check your institution

The easiest way to confirm your brokerage is supported:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Start the connection process">
    Begin linking an account on [tendrill.ai](https://tendrill.ai)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for your institution">
    Type your brokerage name in the search box
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select from results">
    If your institution appears, it's supported. Click to connect.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
**Example search:** Type "fidel" and you'll see results like:
- Fidelity Investments
- Fidelity NetBenefits (401k)
- Fidelity Charitable

Can't find your institution? [Contact Support](/reference/support).
</Tip>

## Connecting multiple accounts

You can link accounts from multiple brokerages to get a complete view of your portfolio:

<Info>
**Aggregate everything.** Connect your Fidelity IRA, Robinhood trading account, and 401(k) - Tendrill will show you the big picture across all accounts.
</Info>

Benefits of connecting multiple accounts:
- See total portfolio value and performance
- Understand overall allocation and diversification
- Get alerts for any holding, regardless of which account it's in
- Compare performance across accounts

## Troubleshooting connections

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="My brokerage doesn't appear in search">
    - Try alternate names (e.g., "Schwab" vs "Charles Schwab")
    - Check if your institution was recently acquired or renamed
    - Some smaller credit unions may not be supported
    - [Contact support](/reference/support) if you believe your institution should be available
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Connection failed after entering credentials">
    - Verify your username and password are correct
    - Check if your brokerage requires a security key or authenticator app
    - Some institutions have temporary outages - try again later
    - See our [connection troubleshooting guide](/connecting-accounts/troubleshooting)
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Some accounts didn't sync">
    - Certain account types (like 529 plans) may not sync holdings
    - Restricted accounts may show limited data
    - Try disconnecting and reconnecting the institution
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
