## You're always in control

Whether you want to disconnect one account, remove all connections, or delete your entire Tendrill account, the process is straightforward. Here's how to manage your data.

## Options available

| Action | What It Does | Your Data |
|--------|--------------|-----------|
| **Disconnect one account** | Removes one brokerage | That account's data deleted |
| **Disconnect all accounts** | Removes all brokerages | All portfolio data deleted |
| **Delete Tendrill account** | Removes everything | Complete data deletion |

## Disconnecting a single account

If you want to remove one brokerage but keep using Tendrill:

### Via dashboard

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to tendrill.ai and log in">
    Navigate to your account settings
  </Step>
  <Step title="Find Connected Accounts">
    Click on the account you want to remove
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click 'Disconnect'">
    Confirm when prompted
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Via text

```
You: Disconnect my Fidelity account

Tendrill: Are you sure you want to disconnect your Fidelity 
account? I'll stop monitoring those holdings.

Type "yes" to confirm.
```

### What happens next

- **Immediately**: Tendrill loses access to that account
- **Within 24 hours**: All data from that account is deleted
- **Alerts**: Any alerts for stocks only in that account are cancelled
- **Other accounts**: Unaffected

## What gets deleted

### Immediately upon disconnection

- Access to your brokerage account(s)
- Real-time monitoring stops
- No new data collected

### Within 24 hours

- Holdings and positions data
- Account balances
- Transaction history
- Active alerts for removed positions

### Within 30 days

- Backup copies are purged
- Audit logs (anonymized) may remain for security

### Preserved unless you request deletion

- Conversation history (can be deleted separately)
- Anonymized usage statistics

## After deletion

### What you'll experience

- No more texts from Tendrill
- Cannot log in to dashboard
- No access to historical insights

### If you want to return

- Sign up fresh with your phone number
- Reconnect your accounts
- Start building history again

We don't penalize returning users ‚Äî you're always welcome back!

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How long does deletion take?">
    - Active data: Within 24 hours
    - Backup copies: Within 30 days
    - You'll receive confirmation when complete
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I get my data back after deletion?">
    No. Deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Make sure you really want to delete before confirming.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Will my brokerage know I disconnected?">
    No. Disconnecting from Tendrill doesn't affect your brokerage account in any way. The connection was read-only and one-directional.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What if I just want to pause?">
    Instead of deleting, you can:
    - Text "pause alerts" to stop notifications temporarily
    - Disconnect accounts but keep your Tendrill account
    - Resume anytime without losing settings
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
