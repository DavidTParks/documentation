## Alerts tailored to you

While Tendrill automatically monitors your portfolio, you can also set up custom alerts for specific conditions you care about. Whether it's a price target, news about a particular stock, or a regular check-in, Smart Alerts keep you informed on your terms.

<Info>
**Set it and forget it.** Create an alert once, and Tendrill will monitor 24/7 until your condition is met.
</Info>

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do alerts work after hours?">
    Price alerts only trigger during regular market hours (9:30 AM - 4:00 PM ET). News alerts work 24/7.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What happens if the market gaps past my alert level?">
    You'll still get notified. If you set an alert at $170 and the stock opens at $165, you'll get an alert when the market opens.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Is there a limit to how many alerts I can set?">
    Free tier: 5 active alerts. Premium: Unlimited alerts.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do alerts expire?">
    Price and news alerts stay active until triggered or cancelled. Intraday movement alerts expire at market close.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
