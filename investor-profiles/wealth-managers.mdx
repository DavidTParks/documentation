## Serve your clients better

As a wealth manager, you're juggling multiple client portfolios with different risk profiles, investment goals, and attention requirements. Tendrill helps you stay on top of everything - alerts across all accounts, quick client-ready insights, and the ability to proactively reach out before clients call you.

<Info>
**Know before your clients ask.** When a holding moves significantly, you'll hear about it first - so you can reach out proactively, not reactively.
</Info>
