## Natural language scheduling

| What You Say | What Happens |
|--------------|--------------|
| "Update me every morning" | Daily at 9:00 AM (default) |
| "Weekly recap on Fridays" | Every Friday at 6:00 PM |
| "Send a summary every Sunday at 7pm" | Sundays at 7:00 PM |
| "Monthly report on the 15th" | 15th of each month |
| "Update me twice a week" | Mondays and Thursdays |
| "Brief me every weekday before market open" | Mon-Fri at 9:00 AM |

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What time zone are updates based on?">
    Updates are sent based on the time zone you registered with. Market-related times (like "before market open") are always in Eastern Time.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Can I get updates on weekends?">
    Yes! Weekend updates summarize the week and preview the upcoming week. Markets are closed, so there's no live data.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="What if I miss an update?">
    Updates aren't stored for later delivery. If you miss one, just ask "give me my daily brief" or "weekly recap" for an on-demand version.
  </Accordion>
  
  <Accordion title="Do recurring tasks count against my message limit?">
    On the free tier, recurring task messages count toward your monthly limit. Premium users have unlimited messages.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
