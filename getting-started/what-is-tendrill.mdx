## Your personal AI wealth manager

Tendrill is an AI-powered assistant that connects to your brokerage accounts and proactively monitors your portfolio for important events. Instead of checking multiple apps or sifting through market noise, you receive personalized, actionable insights delivered straight to your phone via text message.

## FAQ

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Is Tendrill free?">
    Tendrill offers both free and premium tiers. The free tier includes basic portfolio monitoring and limited conversations. Premium unlocks unlimited conversations, advanced alerts, and priority support.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What brokerages are supported?">
    Tendrill supports over 10,000 financial institutions through Plaid, including Fidelity, Charles Schwab, TD Ameritrade, Robinhood, E*TRADE, and many more. [See the full list](/getting-started/supported-brokerages).
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How is my data protected?">
    We use Plaid for secure connections (the same technology used by Venmo, Coinbase, and major banks). We never see your login credentials and maintain a zero data retention policy. [Learn more about security](/privacy-security/data-protection).
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
